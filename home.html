About Us

Tuna Printing & Publishing & Packaging Company ('TUNA' company) It was founded on June 18, 1998. The company is currently one of the leading enterprises in Azerbaijan in the field of paper, packaging and plastic production. Due to its achievements in a short period of time, the quality of its services and the high satisfaction of its customers, 'TUNA' company has taken a worthy place among the leading companies and organizations of Azerbaijan in this sector. Milenium has been cooperating with AER, Shirin, Veysaloglu and other leading companies for many years.
